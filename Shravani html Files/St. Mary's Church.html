St. Mary's Church

Discover the beauty and history of St. Mary’s Church, a serene sanctuary in Pune.

About St. Mary’s Church

St. Mary's Church is one of the oldest churches in Pune, built in 1825 during the British era. Known for its Gothic-style architecture and peaceful ambiance, the church attracts visitors with its stunning stained-glass windows, serene prayer hall, and well-maintained gardens. It is a spiritual haven and a historical landmark, making it a must-visit destination in Pune.

Highlights

St. Mary's Church is celebrated for its architectural and spiritual highlights:

  • Architectural Beauty: Gothic-style design with intricate carvings and high ceilings.
  • Stained-Glass Windows: Depicting biblical scenes and adding vibrant colors to the interiors.
  • Peaceful Environment: A perfect spot for reflection and prayer amidst the bustling city.

Whether you're a history enthusiast or seeking peace, St. Mary’s Church offers a unique blend of spirituality and heritage.

Location

St. Mary’s Church is located in Pune Cantonment, Maharashtra, and is easily accessible by road.

Visit Us

St. Mary's Church is open to visitors throughout the year. It is especially popular during Christmas when the church is beautifully decorated.

Timings: Open daily from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
