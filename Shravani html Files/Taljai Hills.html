Taljai Hills

Experience the tranquility and natural beauty of Taljai Hills, Pune's hidden gem.

About Taljai Hills

Taljai Hills is a popular nature spot in Pune, offering scenic walking trails, panoramic views, and a serene atmosphere. It is a favored destination for morning joggers, nature enthusiasts, and bird watchers. The area is home to a small temple dedicated to Goddess Taljai and is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle.

Highlights

Taljai Hills offers a blend of natural beauty and peaceful surroundings. Key attractions include:

  • Nature Trails: Perfect for walking, jogging, and trekking amidst greenery.
  • Bird Watching: A haven for spotting various bird species, especially in the mornings.
  • Temple: A small yet serene temple dedicated to Goddess Taljai, adding a spiritual touch to your visit.

The hills are especially beautiful during sunrise and monsoon, when the greenery is at its peak.

Location

Taljai Hills is located in the Sahakarnagar area of Pune, Maharashtra, and is easily accessible by road.

Visit Us

Taljai Hills is open throughout the year, offering a refreshing escape into nature. The best time to visit is early morning or evening, especially during the monsoon season.

Timings: Open daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
