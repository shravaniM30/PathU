Saras Bagh

Discover the lush greenery and serene ambiance of Saras Bagh, a beloved garden in Pune.

About Saras Bagh

Saras Bagh is a well-known garden located in the heart of Pune, featuring vibrant green lawns, flowerbeds, and a calming atmosphere. Built during the Peshwa era, Saras Bagh is home to the famous Ganpati Temple, attracting visitors for both spiritual and recreational purposes. The garden is an ideal spot for picnics, evening strolls, and relaxation.

Highlights

Saras Bagh offers a blend of history, spirituality, and leisure. Highlights include:

  • Ganpati Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located within the garden.
  • Lush Greenery: Perfect for picnics and evening walks.
  • Scenic Beauty: Beautifully landscaped garden with a serene pond and vibrant flora.

The garden is a favorite among locals and tourists, especially during festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi.

Location

Saras Bagh is located near Swargate in Pune, Maharashtra, and is easily accessible by road.

Visit Us

Saras Bagh is open to visitors throughout the year. It is a popular destination for families, couples, and groups looking for a peaceful escape within the city.

Timings: Open daily from 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
