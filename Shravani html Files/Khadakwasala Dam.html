About Khadakwasala Dam

Khadakwasala Dam, located near Pune, is a prominent water reservoir known for its picturesque surroundings. The dam is built on the Mutha River and plays a vital role in supplying water to Pune. The area around the dam is a favorite among locals and tourists for picnics, photography, and peaceful getaways, especially during the monsoon season when the dam overflows, creating stunning views.

Highlights

Khadakwasala Dam is popular for its scenic beauty and offers several attractions:

  • Lakefront: Perfect for picnics and relaxing by the water.
  • Sunset Views: Enjoy stunning sunsets over the dam, making it a photographer's delight.
  • Nearby Attractions: Close to Sinhagad Fort and Panshet Dam, making it part of a scenic route.
