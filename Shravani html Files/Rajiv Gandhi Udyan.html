About Rajiv Gandhi Udyan

Rajiv Gandhi Udyan, located in the heart of Pune, is a beautifully maintained garden offering lush greenery, vibrant flowers, and serene walking trails. Ideal for morning walks, family outings, or simply relaxing, this urban park provides a refreshing escape from the hustle of city life. The park is also equipped with a playground, an amphitheater, and a jogging track, making it a hub of recreational activities.

Highlights

Rajiv Gandhi Udyan offers a variety of attractions for visitors of all ages:

  • Floral Beauty: Admire the vibrant flower beds and beautifully landscaped gardens.
  • Play Areas: A dedicated space for children to play and enjoy outdoor activities.
  • Walking Trails: Serene pathways for a peaceful walk or jog amidst nature.
  • Amphitheater: An open-air venue for cultural and recreational events.
