About Rajgad Trek

Rajgad Fort, standing tall at 4,514 feet, served as the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj for over 26 years. The fort is renowned for its breathtaking views, fascinating history, and thrilling trekking routes. Its structures like the Balekilla, Padmavati Machi, and Sanjeevani Machi attract adventurers and history enthusiasts alike.

Highlights

The Rajgad Trek is a must-do experience for anyone seeking adventure and historical exploration. Highlights include:

  • Balekilla: A citadel offering panoramic views of the surrounding Sahyadri ranges.
  • Sanjeevani Machi: A brilliant example of ancient defensive architecture.
  • Padmavati Machi: A plateau featuring ancient ruins and water reservoirs.

The fort is especially beautiful during monsoons when the lush greenery and misty hills create a magical atmosphere.

Location

Rajgad Fort is located approximately 60 km from Pune, Maharashtra, and can be accessed via the base villages of Gunjavane or Pali.
