About Mulshi Dam

Mulshi Dam is a picturesque water reservoir located near Pune, surrounded by the lush greenery of the Sahyadri ranges. Built across the Mula River, the dam is a popular getaway for nature lovers and peace seekers. Its tranquil environment and breathtaking views make it an ideal spot for picnics, photography, and relaxation.

Highlights

Mulshi Dam offers a perfect escape into nature, combining scenic landscapes with a tranquil atmosphere. The backwaters of the dam provide a stunning view of the surrounding mountains and dense forests, creating an ideal spot for photography enthusiasts and adventure seekers.

The area is also known for bird watching and trekking opportunities. During the monsoon season, the lush greenery, cascading waterfalls, and mist-covered hills add to the charm, making it a must-visit destination near Pune.

Visit Us

Mulshi Dam is a year-round destination, with the monsoon season being the best time to visit for its lush greenery and full reservoir. The dam is easily accessible by road and offers a peaceful retreat for families, couples, and groups looking to unwind amidst nature.
